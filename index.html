Unciv, the ultimate adptation of the Civilization V game, but all for mobile and build for potato devices. Create an empire and rule the world however you want. Be the first to the moon, conquer the continent, or be the most civilized of all. Survive the hords of uncivilized barbarians and fend of attacks of your enemies seeking to partition you. You can simulate precisly that, civilization, with this game. If you are for more, the charming community and the variety of mods you can use, will not let you get bored.
